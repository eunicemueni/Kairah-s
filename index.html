Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Fame Booster?

Fame Booster is an advanced AI optimization tool that increases the visibility and engagement of your videos on social media platforms. Included in Diamond, Cinematic, and Lifetime plans, and can also be purchased separately for $9. Free and Pro plans do not have access.

2. How does the referral/affiliate program work?

Every user gets a unique referral code. When someone signs up using your code and makes a payment, you earn 30% commission of that payment. After 100 successful sales through your referrals, you receive a $500 lifetime bonus.

3. Can I refund my payment?

All payments are non-refundable and non-negotiable. Please review your plan and payment method carefully before purchasing.

4. What types of videos can I create?

Kairah Studio allows you to create Text-to-Video, Image-to-Video, and AI Audio content. Each plan has limits on video length and monthly generation, with Diamond, Cinematic, and Lifetime offering unlimited access.

5. How long does video generation take?

Video generation usually takes 10-30 seconds for short clips (up to 60s). Longer videos (180-300s) may take up to 1-2 minutes depending on server load.

6. Are my videos saved?

Yes. All videos you generate are stored in your account and visible in the Video Gallery. You can download them anytime.

7. What payment methods are available?

Kairah Studio supports MPesa, Wise, PayPal, Visa, and Mastercard. Free users don’t need any payment. Paid plans display payment icons once you click “Buy Now”.

8. How do I upgrade my plan?

Click “Buy Now” under your desired plan, select your preferred payment method, and complete the transaction. Your account will automatically update to the new plan.

9. Is my personal information secure?

Yes. All data is securely stored and encrypted. We never share your personal information or AI-generated content without your consent.

10. Can I use Kairah Studio for commercial projects?

Absolutely. All paid plans allow commercial use of your AI-generated content. Free plan content is for personal use only.
